About Us

"Excellence in Education"

Global Matriculation Higher Secondary School, founded in 2010, has never failed to demonstrate its spirit of constant search for excellence in every dimension relevant to the needs of the time. It is marching towards its 13th year. Our school is a co-educational, English medium school imparting holistic and disciplinary education.

Mission Statement

We believe each child is special and our goal is to bring out the best in every child. At Global, we create an environment where a committed management, a team of trained, passionate and dedicated teachers along with the support of the parents are able to focus on the child's all-round development. We accomplish this through a challenging and accommodative curriculum, including sports and extra-curricular activities.
